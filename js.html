Comparison Operators

Comparison Operators
Comparison operators
Operator Returns true if: Examples returning true
Equal (==) operands are equal. 3 == var1
"3" == var1
3 == '3'
Not equal (!=) operands are not equal. var1 != 4
var2 != "3"
Strict equal (===) operands are equal and of the same type. 3 === var1
Strict not equal (!==) operands are of the same type but not equal, or are of different type. var1 !== "3"
3 !== '3'
Greater than (>) left operand is greater than the right operand. var2 > var1
"12" > 2
Greater than or equal (>=) left operand is greater than or equal to the right operand. var2 >= var1
var1 >= 3
Less than (<) left operand is less than the right operand. var1 < var2
"2" < 12
Less than or equal (<=) left operand is less than or equal to the right operand. var1 <= var2
var2 <= 5
